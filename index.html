The Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of point to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on the crystal.

Each time the game starts the values of each crystal will change aswell.
